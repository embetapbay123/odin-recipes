Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a classic Italian pasta dish known for its long, thin noodles and rich tomato-based sauce. It's a versatile and hearty meal that's popular worldwide. This version features a savory meat sauce made with ground beef, aromatic herbs, and garlic, making it perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 300g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions, then drain.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Sauté the chopped onion and garlic until fragrant and soft.
  4. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  5. Stir in the tomato sauce, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  6. Let the sauce simmer for 15–20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Serve the meat sauce over the cooked spaghetti and top with Parmesan if desired.
