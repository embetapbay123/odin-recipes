Beef Pho

Description

Aromatic and comforting Vietnamese noodle soup made with a rich, slow-cooked beef broth, fresh herbs, and tender slices of beef.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef bones
  • 1 onion, halved
  • 1 piece ginger, sliced
  • 2 star anise pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 8 ounces rice noodles
  • 1/2 pound thinly sliced beef sirloin
  • Fresh herbs (cilantro, basil)
  • Lime wedges

Steps

  1. Roast beef bones, onion, and ginger in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for 30 minutes.
  2. Transfer roasted bones, onion, and ginger to a large pot. Add star anise, cinnamon stick, and beef broth. Simmer for 3 hours, skimming off any impurities.
  3. Strain the broth and return it to the pot. Keep warm.
  4. Cook rice noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. To serve, divide noodles among bowls. Top with thinly sliced beef sirloin. Ladle hot broth over the top.
