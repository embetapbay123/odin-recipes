Banh Mi

Description

A Vietnamese sandwich made with a crispy baguette and filled with various meats and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sriracha sauce
  • 1/4 pound cooked pork, sliced thinly
  • 1/4 cup pickled carrots and daikon radish
  • Fresh cilantro leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Split the baguette lengthwise and toast it in the oven for 5 minutes.
  3. Mix the mayonnaise, soy sauce, and sriracha sauce in a small bowl.
  4. Spread the sauce on the toasted baguette.
  5. Layer the sliced pork on top of the sauce.
  6. Add the pickled vegetables and cilantro leaves.
  7. Serve immediately.
