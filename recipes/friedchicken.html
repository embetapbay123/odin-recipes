Fried Chicken

Description

This fried chicken recipe delivers perfectly crispy skin and juicy meat with every bite. Seasoned with a blend of spices and deep-fried to golden perfection, it's a timeless favorite for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk or milk
  • Cooking oil for frying

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour with salt, pepper, and spices.
  2. In another bowl, whisk eggs with buttermilk.
  3. Dip chicken pieces into the egg mixture, then coat with the flour mixture.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  5. Fry chicken until golden brown and fully cooked (about 10â€“15 minutes).
  6. Place on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite sides.
